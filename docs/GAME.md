# How BeatM ROAST MODE Works
## The Game Explained

---

## In One Sentence

**ROAST MODE is a 1v1 trash-talk battle where two players submit Daily Fantasy lineups and an AI judge generates a brutally personalized roast card — turning a solo statistical exercise into a social spectacle.**

---

## Why It's a Game (Not Just a Tool)

| Game Element | How ROAST MODE Has It |
|---|---|
| **Players** | Two rivals — friends, coworkers, group chat enemies |
| **Stakes** | Public bragging rights · the card is screenshot-worthy and shareable |
| **Skill** | Lineup construction — pick players the AI will roast favorably (or hilariously) |
| **Surprise** | The AI's tone shifts night to night · you don't know how hard it'll hit |
| **Resolution** | A verdict tag (`FIRE` / `ICE` / `CHAOS` / `CLASSIC`) + an AI winner pick |
| **Replay** | Lose tonight? Build a revenge lineup tomorrow. The cycle never ends. |

---

## The 5-Beat Loop (under 60 seconds end-to-end)

```
1. CHALLENGE   Drop a ROAST link in a group chat
                "Bet your lineup against mine"

2. SUBMIT      Both players paste their 5-player lineups
                (any sport, any positions — names only)

3. ROAST       Press the button. Claude generates the card
                in ~3 seconds.

4. REACT       Read it live. The reaction — laughter, outrage,
                denial — IS the moment. (This is the demo video.)

5. SHARE       Card lives at a shareable URL. Drop it back
                in the group chat. Friends pile on.
```

Designed to fit **between two plays of a real NBA game.**

---

## What the Player Controls

- **The lineup** — Who they're backing tonight (1–10 player names, any sport)
- **The opponent** — Who they challenge (friend, coworker, rival group chat)
- **The share** — Whether they double down by posting publicly, or quietly take the L

## What the AI Controls

- **The card content** — Headline · two roasts · prediction · crowd pulse · verdict
- **The tone** — Funny, not mean. Punches up at the picks, not the people.
- **The verdict tag** — `FIRE` (high-scoring) · `ICE` (defensive grind) · `CHAOS` (mismatch) · `CLASSIC` (tense + even)

## What's NOT in the MVP (deliberately)

These are Phase 2 mechanics that turn a viral moment into a daily habit:

- ❌ Community voting (which roast was harder?)
- ❌ Leaderboards (who gets roasted most? who roasts hardest?)
- ❌ Streaks (5 wins in a row = legend status)
- ❌ Real-game resolution (does the AI's prediction come true?)
- ❌ User accounts (everything is anonymous URL state)

Phase 2 is **Dirty 30 Blackjack** — a deeper game loop with real stakes, hands you build over a game, and a resolution at the final whistle. The primitives ROAST MODE ships today (Claude card generator, sports data layer, shareable URLs) become Dirty 30's foundation next week.
