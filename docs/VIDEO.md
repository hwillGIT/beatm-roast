## Sofia's 3 Rules for Hackathon Demo Videos

**Rule 1: Show, don't explain.**
The moment you start explaining what the product does with your voice, you've lost. Show someone using it and reacting to it. The brain fills in the rest.

**Rule 2: The worst thing you can do is be boring.**
A polished, slow, feature-walkthrough demo is death. Judges watch 20 of these. The one that makes them feel something wins. Get a laugh on camera. Get a genuine reaction. That's your whole movie.

**Rule 3: One take is usually the best take.**
The most natural moments happen in the first take before people start performing. Shoot fast. Don't over-direct. If someone laughs at the roast card on take 1, that's your cut. Stop shooting.
